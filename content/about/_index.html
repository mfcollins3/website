--- title: About Me ---

My name is Michael Collins, or professionally as Michael F. Collins, III. I am a software developer and engineer with three decades of experience in professional software development, product development, and technology consulting. I am currently the Senior Director for Application Innovation for Neudesic's Desert region serving the Phoenix and Las Vegas markets. In my role, I lead and manages Neudesic's largest practice in terms of employees and revenue. At our peak, I managed 60 full-time software developers. As director, I am responsible for revenue generation and am regularly involved in managing successful project delivery and working with our sales teams to build and develop new client relationships.

My areas of expertise are in custom application or product development utilizing modern front-end and cloud technologies. On the front-end, I have experience developing front-end applications for mobile devices, web browsers, and desktops. On the backend, my expertise is in distributed microservices running in cloud-native environment or orchestration engines using Kubernetes. I work primarily with Microsoft Azure and Amazon Web Services. I have significant experience in utilizing serverless cloud technologies to rapidly build complex distributed services that can elastically scale to handle increasing demand and workloads. I am also very experienced in building event-driven architectures that can handle thousands to millions of messages with no message loss and failure recovery.

I currently live in Surprise, Arizona, at the northwest boundary of the Phoenix metropolitan area. I am married to my wife, Mea, and we have three beautiful children, two dogs, and a really big house that we built during the COVID-19 pandemic and moved into finally in February 2023.
